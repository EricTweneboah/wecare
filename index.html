About Us

Community-driven charity organization

We are a non-profit, community-driven charity organization dedicated to supporting orphans and widows across Ghana. Founded on the principles of compassion, inclusion, and collective responsibility, our organization works closely with local communities to address the social and economic challenges faced by vulnerable families.

Through education support, skills training, livelihood empowerment, healthcare assistance, and community development initiatives, we aim to break cycles of poverty and dependency. We believe that sustainable change is achieved not through charity alone, but through empowering people to become self-reliant and active contributors to their communities. Together with partners, volunteers, and supporters, we are building stronger communities where care, opportunity, and hope are shared by all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to uplift orphans and widows in Ghana by providing care, protection, education, skills development, and sustainable support systems that restore dignity, inspire hope, and create opportunities for a better future. We are committed to empowering vulnerable individuals and strengthening communities through compassion, collaboration, and long-term solutions.

Our Vision

Our vision is a Ghana where orphans and widows are empowered, supported, and fully integrated into thriving communities where every individual has access to opportunity, self-reliance, and a life of dignity regardless of their circumstances.

Leadership & Governance

Leadership team and executive board

Dennis Kofi Sunday - Founder
Dennis Kofi Sunday founded the organization with a strong passion for service and a clear vision to create meaningful and lasting change within communities. His leadership is driven by empathy, accountability, and a deep commitment to empowering those in need. As Founder, Dennis provides strategic direction and ensures that the organization remains true to its mission and values.

Daniella Nkansah - Co-Founder
Daniella Nkansah co-founded the organization alongside Dennis, bringing insight, dedication, and a shared commitment to social responsibility. She plays a key role in shaping the organization's programs and long-term goals, contributing to both strategic planning and community engagement.

Michael Yeboah Asiamah - Operations Director
Michael Yeboah Asiamah oversees the day-to-day operations of the organization, ensuring that projects and initiatives are delivered efficiently and effectively. His role focuses on operational planning, coordination, and implementation, helping to translate the organization's vision into practical and measurable impact.
